Vine Park provieds a unique brewery experience where you can brew your own craft beer or make your own wine.

Whether with friends, co-workers or going solo, Vine Park delivers a unique adventure and experience unlike any other restaurant and brewery. As the only custom craft brewery in the area, we offer one-of-a-kind opportunities to brew three cases of your own craft beer. And let me tell you a secret, beer that you brew yourself tastes the best and don’t worry—our Award-Winning Brewers will guide you through the process to handcraft your beer.  After you finish brewing your own beer, you will come back in 2 to 3 weeks when it’s time to bottle and add your own custom label to your creation.
